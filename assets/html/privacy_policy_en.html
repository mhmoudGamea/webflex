We may collect information about customer activities on websites, applications, cloud services, products and other services. If we combine non-personal information with personal information, the combined information will be treated as personal information as long as it remains combined.

We make it easy to keep your personal information accurate, complete and up to date. We will retain your personal information for the period necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy unless a longer retention period is permitted or required by law.

Location-Based Services

To provide location-based services for our programs, we may collect and use precise location data, including the real-time geographic location of your Waqool Technology device or computer.
